Powerful Features

EduConnect offers a comprehensive set of tools to enhance the learning experience for students, teachers, and administrators.

Live Virtual Classes

Schedule and conduct live classes with integrated video conferencing. Automated reminders and countdown timers ensure no one misses a class.

Smart Attendance System

Automated attendance tracking with camera recognition and geolocation verification. Visual analytics help identify attendance patterns.

AI Performance Prediction

Machine learning algorithms predict student performance based on attendance, assignments, and quiz scores to identify at-risk students early.

Assignment Management

Create, distribute, and grade assignments efficiently. Students can submit work online and receive timely feedback with annotation tools.

Performance Analytics

Comprehensive analytics dashboards provide insights into student progress, engagement levels, and learning outcomes across all courses.

Gamification

Engage students with badges, leaderboards, and rewards. Turn learning into an adventure with quests and achievements that motivate progress.
